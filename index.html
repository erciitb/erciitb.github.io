Have you ever wanted to design a circuit by yourself or control your own bot? Well, you’ve come to the right place! Electronics and Robotics Club, an integral part of the Institute Technical Council, IIT Bombay, is the club that satiates the techie that you all possess. Our vision is to develop a powerful and remarkable technical community of students across all years and departments. We encourage every student to delve into tech and also provide you with a plethora of opportunities- be it, group discussions, hackathons, boot camps, GCs, expert-talks, or even various competitions. The Electronics and Robotics Club was born from the two of its parent clubs(guess which? :P), 2017 being the year of christening. We encourage every one of you to tinker around and excel in your technical skills by performing hands-on experiments with the innumerable resources at your disposal. ERC is ever-ready to welcome new innovative ideas, contributions as well as suggestions for its excellence. A perfect place for you to explore tech and tinker around!
Main Events

XLR8

Race to finish line!

Your mission, should you choose to accept it, is to design a remote-controlled bot from scratch. Using your phone, you should be able to manoeuvre the bot safely through a track filled with a diverse set of obstacles. Be warned; The clock is ticking!

Intro to Arduino

Ar-do-we-know?

We organise the Arduino Bootcamp to get you started with programming in Arduino. Have no prior programming experience? No worries again, we’ll start from scratch and show you the power of Arduino with hands-on experience in the session.

Line Follower

Be on the line!

Remember that awesome bot you made during XLR8? Now it's time to teach your bot to think and make its own decisions!
Now you have a modified mission- build an autonomous robot that can detect and follow a white path on a black backdrop-using the Arduino microcontroller!

Get Electrified!!

A trip to electronics.

Really informative & fun hands-on sessions where we will give you an introduction to analog and digital electronics. In the analog session, we will implement circuits and see actual waveforms.

Key Projects

Drone Delivery

New generation

Automized Drone which can deliver essential goods and products from one location to another in the Covid-19 Pandemic.
  • Simulation of a multi-drone system in gazebo.
  • Capable of handling simultaneous deliveries with multiple drones.

Wall climbing Bot

The Fifth Sense

A wall-climbing robot to perform a smooth floor-to-wall transition, stick to the wall using the lift force generated by the propeller attached to the BLDC motor and navigate seamlessly on the surface of the wall by detecting obstacles.

Robotex Line
Follower

LEGO!

The task was to make the Fastest Line Follower. .The PS was solved by the team by implementing PID and making bot as light-weighted as possible. Keeping this in mind , the team chose Pololu Micro Motors for its weight , high RPM and small size.

Smart Suitcase

Style meets comfort

Smarty is a next-gen suitcase which contacts you whenever and wherever you want. Smarty will follow you around and prevent your suitcase from being robbed and also will feel you burden free xD!
  • Hands-Free Control
  • Smart Obstacle Avoidance
  • Intelligent Tracking
