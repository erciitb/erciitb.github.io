Hello there! Greetings from Electronics & Robotics Club IIT Bombay! We cater to every individual who ever wanted to design their own circuit and control their own bot.

We are a club that satiates the techie you all possess! As a club, we organize events like Hackathons, Competitions, Workshops, Group Discussions, etc. throughout the year. We also have tutorials, blogs and a wiki, and we are ALWAYS open for contributions.
Main Events

XLR8

A race against time

You are now a member of a team of four. Your mission, should you choose to accept it, is to design a remote-controlled bot from scratch. Using your phone, you should be able to manoeuvre the bot safely through a track filled with a diverse set of obstacles. Be warned; The clock is ticking!

Arduino Bootcamp

Ar-do-we-know?

ERC organises Arduino Bootcamp to get you started with Arduino programming. Have no prior programming experience? No worries, we’ll start from scratch and show you the power of Arduino with hands-on experience in the session.With this kickstart, and some practise of your own, you’ll be creating your own Arduino projects in no time!

A fROSty Winter

ROS Workshop

Are you ready to help Sherlock? Join us for 'fROSty Winter', a 4-week workshop on 'Robot Operating System (ROS)', which is used in most state-of-the-art robots around the world today. The workshop kicks off on 1st December.

Line Follower

Dare to Deviate

Remember that awesome bot you made during XLR8? Now it's time to teach your bot to think! Modified mission- build a bot that can detect and follow a white path on a black backdrop-using the Arduino microcontroller. This will introduce you to microcontrollers along with a flavour of control systems.

Get Electrified!!

as simple as 01,10,11

Throughout the year, we conduct several Get Electrified sessions where we will give you an introduction to analog and digital electronics. We will implement circuits and see actual waveforms and learn their amazing applications. Finally, using all the things discussed, you shall be building a cool project!

Key Projects

Drone Delivery

New generation

Automized Drone which can deliver essential goods and products from one location to another in the Covid-19 Pandemic.
  • Simulation of a multi-drone system in gazebo.
  • Capable of handling simultaneous deliveries with multiple drones.

Wall climbing Bot

The Fifth Sense

A wall-climbing robot to perform a smooth floor-to-wall transition, stick to the wall using the lift force generated by the propeller attached to the BLDC motor and navigate seamlessly on the surface of the wall by detecting obstacles.

Robotex Line
Follower

LEGO!

The task was to make the Fastest Line Follower. .The PS was solved by the team by implementing PID and making bot as light-weighted as possible. Keeping this in mind , the team chose Pololu Micro Motors for its weight , high RPM and small size.

Smart Suitcase

Style meets comfort

Smarty is a next-gen suitcase which contacts you whenever and wherever you want. Smarty will follow you around and prevent your suitcase from being robbed and also will feel you burden free xD!
  • Hands-Free Control
  • Smart Obstacle Avoidance
  • Intelligent Tracking
