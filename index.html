Hello there! Greetings from Electronics & Robotics Club IIT Bombay! We cater to every individual who ever wanted to design their own circuit and control their own bot.

We are a club that satiates the techie you all possess! As a club, we organize events like Hackathons, Competitions, Workshops, Group Discussions, etc. throughout the year. We also have tutorials, blogs and a wiki, and we are ALWAYS open for contributions.
