Main Events

XLR8

A race against time

You are now a member of a team of four. Your mission, should you choose to accept it, is to design a remote-controlled bot from scratch. Using your phone, you should be able to maneuver the bot safely through a track filled with a diverse set of obstacles. Be warned; The clock is ticking!

Arduino Bootcamp

Ar-do-we-know?

Arduino Bootcamp gets you started with Arduino programming. Have no prior programming experience? No worries, we’ll start from scratch and show you the power of Arduino with hands-on experience in the session.With this kickstart, and some practice, you’ll be creating your own Arduino projects in no time!

A fROSty Winter

ROS Workshop

Are you ready to help Sherlock? Join us for 'fROSty Winter', a 4-week workshop on 'Robot Operating System (ROS)', which is used in most state-of-the-art robots around the world today. The workshop kicks off on 1st December.

Line Follower

Dare to Deviate

Remember that awesome bot you made during XLR8? Now it's time to teach your bot to think! Modified mission- build a bot that can detect and follow a white path on a black backdrop using the Arduino microcontroller. This will introduce you to microcontrollers along with a flavor of control systems.

Get Electrified!!

as simple as 01,10,11

Throughout the year, we conduct several Get Electrified sessions where we will give you an introduction to analog and digital electronics. We will implement circuits and see actual waveforms and learn their amazing applications. Finally, using all the things discussed, you shall be building a cool project!
